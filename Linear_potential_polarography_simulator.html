    Why this version is more accurate
    • Polarographic wave height responds to concentration and drop time in the correct direction.
    • Stationary-electrode peak height responds to concentration and approximately to √scan-rate in the correct direction.
    • A mixture preset is included, so students can see two separated half-wave regions in one polarogram.
    • The current axis auto-scales, so oxidation and reduction traces are both displayed realistically.
    Still a teaching simulator, not a finite-element research solver.
